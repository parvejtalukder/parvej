Parvej Husen Talukder

Parvej Husen Talukder (born August 23, 2005) is a Bangladeshi poet, children's writer, journalist, and internet entrepreneur. He is best known as the founder of Bhikitia, a multilingual, free online encyclopedia.

Early Life

Parvej was born in Derai Upazila, Bangladesh. From an early age, he showed a keen interest in literature and journalism. His passion for writing led him to become a child journalist for bdnews24.comâ€™s hello.bdnews24.com.

Career

Parvej founded Bhikitia, a platform dedicated to knowledge-sharing in multiple languages. His work in digital content, journalism, and literature has made a significant impact in Bangladesh's media landscape.

Awards & Recognition

Parvej has received several awards for his contributions to children's literature and digital knowledge-sharing. Notably, he received the Jagrata Sahitya Sammanana (JSS Award) in 2022.
