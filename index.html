Parvej Husen Talukder

Md Parvej Husen Talukder, Mostly known as Parvej Husen Talukder is a Bangladeshi poet, children's literature author, and internet entrepreneur. He is the founder of Bhikitia, a multilingual online encyclopedia, and Kavya Kishor International, a prominent platform for children's literature. He has made notable contributions to literature and digital entrepreneurship, particularly through his works in Bengali and English.

Early Life and Education

Parvej was born in Jatichar, Sunamganj, Bangladesh. He completed his early education at Amira Atif Shishu Kanon and Gochiya Government Primary School. Parvej later attended Gochiya Shamsuddin Sikandar High School, where he completed his JSC (2019) and SSC (2022) exams. He graduated with an HSC degree in 2024 from Sylhet Science and Technology College and is currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Engineering at Sylhet International University.

Literary Career

Parvej began his literary journey with children's literature and is affectionately known as the "King Rhymester of Haor Region" since mid-2021. Some of his notable works include:

  • Chorar Jhalak (Bengali rhymes)
  • ABC's Rhymes
  • Mojar Pora Chondo Chora (Bengali)
  • Saat Nibanda (Bengali)
  • Smrithir Alponay Kavya

Editorial and Organizational Roles

Beyond writing, Parvej has significantly contributed to literary organizations:​

Founder of Kavya Kishor International (KKI):

Established in 2020, KKI is a global literary organization dedicated to nurturing young talent and promoting literary arts. Parvej served as the Chief Executive Editor from 2020 to 2023, before transitioning to the role of Chief Executive Editor in September 2024.

​

Editor of Kavya Kishor:

Under his editorial guidance, the magazine has flourished, providing a platform for emerging writers and poets.

Journalistic Endeavors

Parvej's journalistic pursuits include:​
Children's Journalist at bdnews24.com: From 2021 to 2023, he collaborated with UNICEF to produce content aimed at younger audiences, enriching the digital media landscape.​
Editor of Daily Topnotch: Currently, he oversees editorial operations, ensuring the publication's commitment to quality journalism.

Digital Innovations

Parvej Husen Talukder has made significant strides in digital knowledge-sharing by co-founding WikiGenius and launching Bhikitia, both of which aim to revolutionize online content and information accessibility.

WikiGenius

Launched in 2023, WikiGenius is an open-source, wiki-based platform designed to serve as an alternative to traditional encyclopedias. The site allows users to create and manage their own content while maintaining a flexible notability standard. WikiGenius also features verified editors to ensure accuracy and high-quality content, providing a user-friendly experience. It has become a valuable platform for individuals and businesses to share information and resources.

Bhikitia

In October 2024, Parvej founded Bhikitia, a multilingual, free online encyclopedia that is accessible to everyone. The platform focuses on providing reliable information across various fields, such as history, culture, science, and global issues. With its intuitive design and comprehensive content, Bhikitia has quickly gained recognition as a valuable educational tool. Initially launched as Vikitia, the site was rebranded in mid-2021 to Bhikitia to better reflect its mission to democratize knowledge and foster international collaboration.

Awards & Recognition

Parvej has received several awards for his contributions to children's literature and digital knowledge-sharing. Notably, he received the Jagrata Sahitya Sammanana (JSS Award) in 2022.
